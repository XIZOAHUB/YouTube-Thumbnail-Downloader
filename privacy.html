Privacy Policy

Updated • 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how xizoa’s YouTube Thumbnail Download Tool handles your data. Our goal is simple: we don't collect anything unnecessary, and we don't store what you search.

1. What Data We Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your YouTube URLs
  • Your thumbnails
  • Your IP address for tracking
  • Your personal information

We only use basic server logs required for security and performance. These logs are not used for tracking.

2. How the Tool Works

The tool fetches thumbnails directly from YouTube’s public image servers. Your request is processed on the client-side; nothing is uploaded to us.

3. Cookies

We do not use cookies for analytics, tracking, or advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

If any third-party embedded content (e.g., YouTube iframe previews) appears, their privacy policies apply.

5. Children’s Privacy

This site is safe for all ages and collects no personal data.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

Updates may occur. If changed, the “Updated” badge will be refreshed.

7. Contact

For questions: hello@xizoa.com
